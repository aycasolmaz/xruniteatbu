Welcome to XR Unite @ BU

At XR Unite, we strive to teach the Boston University student body the future possibilities Extended Reality has to offer.  

MISSION

XR Unite seeks to unite students of all different studies through our common interest in XR (Augmented Reality and Virtual Reality). We strive to understand and experience the principles of XR through related webinars, seminars, workshops, and participate in global hackathons to master their skills and bring new and innovative ideas to the Boston University community through different workshops, events, and hands-on industry experiences. Our members will be exposed to XR through a career oriented lens which will ultimately allow them to explore new career opportunities.

Dive into the world of XR with us...

All part-time and full-time undergraduates at Boston University are eligible to join XR Unite.
